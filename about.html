WHERE INNOVATION MEETS RELIABILITY

Communication is a key factor in the majority of global industries. RDTech is the communication provider where Innovation meets Reliability. Therefore, we set out to use the newest technologies in our products, after they pass extensive testing to prove they won’t fail our clients in the field.

ABOUT US

RD TECH is a leading global provider of wireless communication solutions, established in 2017. Since our inception, our mission has been to empower governments, businesses, and individuals by providing them with access to innovative, reliable, and secure technology.
Our team, formed by professional hardware engineers and skilled software developers, is driven by our passion to connect everyone and everything. We develop global communication networks, that are affordable, high-quality, and secure, and that can perform in any terrain and environment, under any conditions - on the ground, at sea, or in the air.
OUR MISSION
We are set on providing businesses from a wide variety of industries with cutting-edge, secure, and reliable wireless communications solutions in order to improve and optimize the productivity, safety, and profitability of their operations. Our commitment is to be a trusted global partner to businesses around the world and deliver innovative software and hardware solutions that fit the unique needs and challenges of our clients, and ensure their success and growth.
OUR VISION
RDTech’s core values are innovation, security, and customer satisfaction. We are driven by our values and beliefs, and consider that access to industry-leading communication solutions is a key factor in improving a business, industry, and even our world. Our hardware and software experts are dedicated to their craft with these values in mind, and aim to provide top-of-the-class technology, software, and customer experience to all our clients.
