NET-RDL2

NET-RDL2

Device Description
An innovative device perfect for a broad range of autonomous applications including UAVs, drones, robotics, and others. Its self-forming and self-healing capabilities allow flawless connectivity. Its tracking antenna terminal is lightweight, easy to deploy, and provides sufficient gain for a range of up to 100km, even with a low-power radio. It also features unlimited frequency hops and intelligent time-based blacklisting algorithms for self-healing.
